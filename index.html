Juan Carlos Urcia Barea

I am a capable and motivated public servant with more than two years of progressively advanced experience in the United States Senate. I currently serve as a Legislative Correspondent advising Senator Ben Cardin of Maryland on labor, welfare, technology, immigration, and civil rights policy in support of the Floor Director and Chief Counsel.

I graduated from Brown University in 2017 with a B.A. in History with a focus on Ancient Rome and Late Antiquity. My interests center on the human world: history, culture, politics, philosophy, and economics. What drives me is the question of how labor and economic policies can empower workers and provide for a more equitable distribution of outcomes.
