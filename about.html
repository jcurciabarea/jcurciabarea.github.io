Welcome. My name is Juan Carlos Urcia Barea. I am a capable and motivated public servant currently based in Washington, DC. I made this website in HTML and CSS in order to share a bit about myself.


I currently serve as a Legislative Correspondent for United States Senator Ben Cardin. I help oversee to advance the Senator's legislative priorities and oversee an issue portfolio of labor, social safety net, technology, immigration, and civil rights policy.


Topics of particular interest are labor and economic policy, tools that can help empower disadvantaged communities and create a more equitable economic distribution. I hope to learn more about collective bargaining and workers' rights, in addition to financial markets, international trade, and monetary and fiscal policy.


I completed my Bachelor of Arts in History at Brown University in 2017, with a concentration on Ancient Rome and Late Antiquity.


My long-term objective is to build an impactful career in federal service while also learning more about the country and the world one step at a time. It has been a very fun experience so far.


I made this website in HTML and CSS in the early months of 2020.
